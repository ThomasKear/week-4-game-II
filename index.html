RULES TO YOUR DOOM

At the beginning of the game you will be assigned a number. By clicking on one of the four skull crystals you add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game when your total score equals the assigned number. You lose if your total score goes over the assigned number.
The values of the crystals change at the beginning of each round.
